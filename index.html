About Me

I’m an interaction designer with over 6 years of experience designing user experiences. I’m passionate about creating innovative and intuitive experiences for others. I love making processes more efficient and enjoyable. I challenge the status quo with out-of-the-box questions and innovative solutions. I solve problems by relentlessly breaking down processes and analyzing data. I’m always learning something new, and love it!

I love crafting wireframes, mockups, prototypes, user flows, site-maps, and designing & engineering front-end experiences.
